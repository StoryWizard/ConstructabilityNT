Project Management

Constructability NT has become a preferred civil and concrete construction contractor within the industry by engaging a workforce that is experienced and professional, considerate of local engagement,developing advanced systems and processes, and adopting an innovative approach to management. With a well equipped fleet of plant & equipment, solid client management techniques and an unwavering commitment to safety, the company consistently delivers a service of the highest quality, on time and within budget. Our team members are the key to our success. From top management down the line, all members share responsibility for ensuring the success of a project and all have a right to feel proud of the end result. Recognising that all projects are unique, we will work closely with our Client on all levels to successfully deliver our commitment.

Health & Safety

Constructability NT is committed to the safety and well being of every stakeholder. Facilitated by senior management, with full engagement from team members, the company safety program is designed to encourage active participation. Prevention of accidents is top priority, and is underpinned by our third party accredited AS4801:2001 compliant Safety Management System. Our 'zero harm' safety objective is reinforced with best practices and systems, driven by a firm dedication to safety as a key element within our company culture.

Environmental

Constructability NT commits to minimising our environmental impact through responsible policy and practice. Diligent monitoring and benchmarking of our third party accredited AS14001:2004 compliant Environmental Management System, accompanied by site-aware best practice, ensures a solid team culture of environmental sustainability and accountability.

Quality

Regardless of project scope, Constructability NT's key objective is to deliver quality and satisfaction to our clients. Our third party accredited AS9001:2008 compliant Quality Management System is integral to our business, ensuring consistently successful outcomes. Our systems are maintained and upgraded via regular auditing for standards and operational relevance, ensuring all team members assume responsibility, with exceptional support and management from senior quality representatives.
