Pre Feasability Planning

We collaboratively engage with clients and utilize our skills and experience in the initial design phase of projects to ensure the foundation design has a defined concrete foundation budget and firm milestone dates for the delivery of concrete foundation elements.

Our approach of early engagement and an overall project prefeasibility assessment ensures significant reduction of financial and time contingencies, with the works execution scale of efficiency ensuring a de-risked project in terms of achieving the most crucial aspect- time.

Our experience within the industry, in particular with concrete works on remote civil projects, provides clients with the surety and confidence the project will be delivered on time and on budget.
